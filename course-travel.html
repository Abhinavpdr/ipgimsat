Diploma in Travel and Tourism (1 Year)

The one-year Diploma in Travel and Tourism program is designed to provide students with a deep understanding of the travel and tourism industry. It covers topics such as tour operations, travel agency management, destination planning, and customer service. Graduates are prepared for roles in travel agencies, tour companies, and tourism management.
